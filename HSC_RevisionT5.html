Prejudice (The Thought)

  • Attitudes or pre-judgements made about others which eventually lead to discrimination.
  • Usually negative attitudes which are often based entirely on ignorance.
  • Having stereotyped views (e.g., blindly believing older people are less intelligent).

Discrimination (The Action)

  • Unfair treatment based on gender, race, sexuality, disability, or beliefs.
  • The active, unfair treatment of a person based on prejudice and intolerance.
  • Denying an individual or a specific group the exact same rights everyone else enjoys.
